Privacy Policy for My Diary

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

My Diary is a personal journaling app. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Storage

All diary entries, passwords, and settings you create in this app are stored only on your own device, using your browser's local storage. No data is sent to, collected by, or stored on any external server. The app developer has no access to any of your diary content, passwords, or personal information.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, analytics, or usage data with the developer or any third party.

No Internet Access Required

Once installed, the app works fully offline. It does not connect to the internet to send or receive any of your data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage/data from your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the app listing page.
